Delete Your Account

Self-service account deletion

You can delete your Tapped account and all associated data directly within the app. This action is permanent and cannot be undone.

How to Delete Your Account in the App

1

Open Tapped on your iPhone.

2

Swipe left to go to the Lists page.

3

Tap the gear icon in the top-right corner to open Settings.

4

Scroll to the bottom and tap “Delete Account”.

5

Confirm the deletion in the alert dialog.

What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed from our servers:

  • Your user profile and email address
  • All tasks, lists, habits, and subtasks
  • All archived items
  • Your subscription status (active subscriptions must be cancelled separately through Apple)

Important: Cancel Your Subscription First

Deleting your account does not automatically cancel your subscription. If you have an active Tapped Pro subscription, please cancel it before deleting your account:

  • Go to your iPhone’s Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions
  • Find Tapped and tap Cancel Subscription

Alternative: Request Deletion via Email

If you are unable to access the app, you can request account deletion by emailing us. Please include the email address associated with your account.

Email: support@tappedapp.xyz

We will process email deletion requests within 30 days.
